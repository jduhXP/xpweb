About Xtreme Precision

More Than a Shop.
A Standard.

We built Xtreme Precision on the belief that auto repair should be straightforward — quality work, fair prices, and no runaround.

🏎️

Our Story

Started in 2008 with a goal of delivering quality service

Founder and Master Technician Bob Duh grew up in his grandfather's garage in Indianapolis, learning that the right repair done right the first time is the only repair that matters. After decades working for different auto shops, he saw how the industry left customers feeling uninformed and overcharged.

He opened Xtreme Precision with a different model. Deliver quality work & customer service, to treat every car as if it was his own.
